# Cultist - Skill Investment Readout

For each spec, every skill that has at least one talent modifying it, and the flat list of talents to invest in for it. Class-tree talents are always available regardless of spec, so they're folded into each spec's list rather than shown separately. Pick the skill you already like, take the talents underneath it.
